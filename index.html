About Sean

I coach people who are done making excuses.

I'm Sean Nygren — a certified personal trainer based in Stockholm. I got into this because I know what it feels like to train hard with no real direction. Once I found science-based programming, everything changed.

My approach is simple: understand your goal, build a programme that fits your life, and execute with consistency. No crash diets, no extreme protocols, no gimmicks. Just progressive, evidence-based training.

Whether you're in Stockholm and want to train in person, or you're anywhere in the world and need remote coaching — I build programmes that actually work.
